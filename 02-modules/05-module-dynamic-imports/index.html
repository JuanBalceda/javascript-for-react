PS5 release

It's official. Sony has just unveiled the official price of its new PlayStation 5: 499.99 euros for the PlayStation 5 with disc player and 399.99 euros for the fully digital version. That way, the Japanese company leaves the more complete model to match the Xbox Series X, while surpassing in price the most affordable model of Microsoft, the Xbox Series S, which stands at 299 euros.
